Dinner and Seminar Event Series

This is a free dinner and seminar series for woman interested in learning about computer science and the many ways it is used in your daily life. During the event, we will discuss topics such as algorithms, control flow, and much more! This is a chance to build a community and interact with current undergraduate student mentors in the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. This research study is led by Dr. Jennifer Parham-Mocello.

Topics

  • What is an Algorithm?
  • Data Representation
  • Control Flow/Logic
  • Decomposition
  • Software Design
