Meet Our Mentors!

  At each event you will have a mentor working with you and discussing the event topics. All mentors are currently undergraduate students here at Oregon State. Feel free to learn more about each of us as well as contact us if you have any questions relating to, attending OSU or our general college experience.


Claudia

I first got interested in CS when I TAed a computer science course in high school. In this course, I got to see a variety of projects being made as well as discovered that CS was applicable to many different fields. I now apply psychology to CS, to help create better user interfaces and improve the user's exerpience. CS has taught me how to think differently about problems I approach and has also helped me learn how to break down problems further, rather than look at them from a big picture. When I am not working on school, research or TAing for the intro CS courses, I like to spend time with my friends and family, watch movies, and read.

Lucy

I'm a Senior in Computer Science here at OSU. I had an idea for a physics-themed website in my senior year of high school, and started learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript in an effort to bring that to fruition. It never actually manifested, but I switched to a CS major that summer and fell in love with programming during CS 160. Computer science has changed the way I look at problems: once you have a hammer, everything is a nail! It opens up options for how you can address issues that you see or improve tools you use, and especially in the Open Source community can help you help others. It's so empowering to contribute to other projects, and know that when someone opens Firefox they're using a little part of the code you wrote. In my free time I enjoy working out, hiking, cooking, and reading.
