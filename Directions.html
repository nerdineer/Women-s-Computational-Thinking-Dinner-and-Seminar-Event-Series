Directions to Campus

All events will be hosted in the Memorial Union building, on campus at Oregon State University. When you arrive at the building there is an information desk right inside. Please use this resource in order to locate the correct room. The room name for each event can be found here.

Since our events will be held from 5:30pm-8:30pm, there is no need to worry about parking costs. Any parking lot on campus is free to park in after 5pm.
